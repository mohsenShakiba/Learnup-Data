Asking for Time Off
off, request, days, approve, annual, unpaid, entitlement, application, superior, authorise, absence, arrangement, temporary, timesheet, deduct, wage, sickness, compassionate, granted, approval
Excuse me, can I talk to you for a minute?
Of course. What is it?
I'd like to request some time off next week.
How many days do you need?
Three days. It's for a family matter.
Is this annual leave or unpaid?
I still have some annual entitlement left.
Good. Fill in a holiday application form.
Do you approve it yourself?
No, your superior must authorise the request.
Will my absence cause problems?
We may be short, but we'll make an arrangement.
Is this a temporary change to my timesheet?
Yes. I'll mark it and won't deduct your wage.
What if it were for sickness instead?
Then it's sick leave, not compassionate leave.
So the days are granted?
Yes, once I get final approval.
Thank you so much.
