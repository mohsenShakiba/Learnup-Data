Talking About the Seasons
leaves, prefer, shorter, sunshine, frost, blossom, harvest, chilly, humid, mild, springtime, wintry, drizzle, sleet, seasonal, daylight, overcast, breezy, crisp, scorching
Which season do you like best?
I prefer summer and its warm sunshine.
Really? I find summer too scorching.
What's your favourite, then?
Autumn. The leaves and the harvest are lovely.
True, but the days grow shorter.
I don't mind the crisp, breezy air.
Do you like winter?
It's often wintry and chilly, with frost and sleet.
And spring?
In springtime the blossom is beautiful.
What about the rain?
A light drizzle is fine, but I hate a grey, overcast sky.
Is the weather mild where you live?
Yes, quite mild and humid in summer.
Do you enjoy the long daylight?
Yes, these seasonal changes are special.
Each season has its own charm.
