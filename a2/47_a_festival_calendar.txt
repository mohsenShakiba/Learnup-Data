A Festival Calendar

You know, our town has a festival almost every month.
Really? When do they start?
The music festival kicks it off in March, with a concert and a local band.
Is there anything before that, in February?
Yeah, a little flower festival in February, with one small crafts stall.
Nice. What about spring and summer?
There's an art fair in April, a book festival in May, then a big one in June with fireworks in July.
Oh, I love a big summer crowd.
Same. And a food festival in August with a street parade, plus a cooking contest.
What about autumn?
One in September and another in October. The October events are in the town hall.
And winter? Anything on the program?
In November there's a show with dancing, and light festivals in December and January.
Do many visitors come for the winter lights?
Yes, and the leaflet shows the opening and closing times.
Wow, so there's really something every month!
