Pitching an Idea
pitch, convince, evidence, rollout, compelling
Thanks for making time. I have an idea to pitch.
I'm listening. What do you propose?
A loyalty program to retain customers.
Interesting. What's the potential benefit?
It could increase repeat purchases by twenty percent.
That's a bold claim. Do you have evidence?
Yes, competitors saw similar results.
My concern is the upfront investment.
The return should cover it within a year.
How would you handle the rollout?
We'd start with a small pilot first.
That's a compelling case. Let's move forward.
