Talking About Ambition
success, fulfilment, define, content, meaningful, relentless
Do you consider yourself ambitious?
I used to be, relentlessly so.
What changed?
I realized I was sacrificing too much.
So you redefined success?
Yes. Now I value fulfilment over status.
But isn't ambition what drives us?
It is, as long as it stays healthy.
I struggle to feel content.
Chasing more endlessly is exhausting.
Maybe I need a healthier perspective.
Ambition is good, but balance is better.
