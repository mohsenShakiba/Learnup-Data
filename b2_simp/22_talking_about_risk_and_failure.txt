Talking About Risk and Failure
failure, setback, resilience, calculated, bounce back, fear, persevere, mindset
Were you ever afraid to start your business?
Of course. The fear of failure was huge.
How did you overcome it?
I took calculated risks, not reckless ones.

It was, but it taught me valuable lessons.
So failure wasn't the end?
Not at all. Resilience is everything.
It's really about the mindset, isn't it?
Exactly. Embrace failure and you'll grow.
