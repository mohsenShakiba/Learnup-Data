The Ethics of Ambition
ruthless, cutthroat, scruples, attain, conscience, corrosive, noble
When does ambition cross an ethical line?
When it starts trampling on others.
So ambition itself isn't the problem?
Not at all. It can be quite noble.
But some pursue success ruthlessly.
Abandoning their principles along the way.
They tell themselves the end justifies it.
A dangerously seductive rationalization.
Can you stay ambitious and principled?
Absolutely, if you keep your conscience intact.
So integrity is the real boundary.
Yes. Success without it rings hollow.
