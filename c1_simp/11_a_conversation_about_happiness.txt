A Conversation About Happiness
happiness, contentment, fleeting, pursuit, meaning, gratitude, elusive, comparison, intrinsic, satisfaction
Do you think happiness can really be pursued?
Oddly, the harder you chase it, the more elusive it becomes.
So it's a by-product rather than a goal?
Exactly. It tends to arise from meaning.
Many confuse it with constant pleasure.
But pleasure is fleeting by nature.
Whereas contentment runs deeper.
I find gratitude shifts my whole perspective.
And comparison tends to poison it.
True. Expectations are often the enemy.
Perhaps it's about being fully present. If I had saved more when I was young, I would be more relaxed now.
Yes, contentment lives in the present moment.
