A Conversation About Wisdom
wisdom, knowledge, discernment, judgment, accumulate, reflection, prudent, insight, distinguish, mature
Is wisdom just accumulated knowledge?
No, knowledge is knowing; wisdom is judging.
So you can be knowledgeable but unwise?
Certainly. Plenty of clever fools exist.
Then how is wisdom actually acquired?
Largely through reflection on experience.
So mistakes are essential to it?
Indispensable, provided we learn from them.
It seems to require real humility.
Yes, knowing the limits of what you know.
That's a discernment few truly master.
Which is why the wise stay endlessly curious.
