A Conversation About Legacy
legacy, enduring, remembered, selfless, ripple, posterity, transcend, matter
Do you ever think about the legacy you'll leave?
More and more, as I get older.
What would you want to be remembered for?
For kindness, more than achievement.
That's a refreshingly humble answer.
Titles fade, but how you treat people endures.
I suppose legacy isn't about fame.
No, it's the quiet ripple we leave behind.
Often we never see its full effect.
True. It transcends our own lifetime.
That makes everyday actions feel significant.
Indeed. Small acts can matter most of all.
