Time and Priorities
finite, intentional, squander, deliberate, scarce
We treat time as if it were limitless.
Yet it's our scarcest resource.
We squander it on trivial things.
Because we rarely pause to reflect.
How do you decide what truly matters?
I ask what I'd regret neglecting.
That's a sobering way to choose.
Every yes is a no to something else.
So priorities are really trade-offs.
Exactly. We must be intentional.
Otherwise life simply drifts past.
Living deliberately is the whole challenge.
