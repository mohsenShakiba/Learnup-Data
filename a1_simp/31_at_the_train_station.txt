At the Train Station
train, express, local, announcement, direct, carriage, compartment, conductor, lounge, waiting, escalator, entrance, trolley, refreshment, exit, railway, speed, tunnel, whistle, rail
Excuse me, I have a ticket to Paris. When is the next train?
It leaves at three o'clock. It's a fast express, not the local one.
Which platform is it?
Platform five. But the train is a little late. Listen for the announcement.
Oh no. How late is it?
About ten minutes. Don't worry.
Is it a direct train, or do I need to change?
You change trains in Lyon. It is not direct.
Which carriage is my seat in?
Carriage four, in a quiet compartment. A conductor will check your ticket.
Where can I wait? Is there a lounge?
Yes, the waiting lounge is up the escalator, near the entrance.
Great. Can I buy a coffee there?
Yes, a trolley sells refreshments, or there's a café by the exit.
How fast does the train go?
The railway is new, so the train has high speed through the long tunnel.
Nice. Thank you. I'll go to the platform now.
When you hear the whistle, the rail doors close. Have a good trip.
