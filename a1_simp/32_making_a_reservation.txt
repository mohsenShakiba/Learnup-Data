Making a Reservation
tonight, check, see, reserve, special, date, occasion, anniversary, romantic, booth, private, starter, main, course, vegetarian, allergy, highchair, host, notice, cancel, confirm
Good evening, Bella Restaurant. How can I help you?
Hello. I'd like to reserve a table for tonight.
Sure. For how many people?
For four people, please. It's a special date.
What is the occasion?
Our wedding anniversary. We want a romantic table.
How lovely! What time would you like?
At eight in the evening.
We have a free table at eight. Would you like a booth or a private corner?
A quiet booth, please. Can we start with a starter?
Of course. Our main courses are excellent too.
One of us is vegetarian and has a nut allergy.
No problem. Do you need a highchair for a child?
Yes, one highchair, please.
The host will greet you at the door. What name is the table under?
Under the name David.
Perfect. Let me check: four people at eight. Please give notice if you cancel.
That's right. I confirm. See you tonight.
