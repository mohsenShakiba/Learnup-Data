At the Post Office
package, send, letter, stamp, heavy, address, fast, country, receipt, add, mail, write, weigh, sender, postcard, envelope, tape, mailbox, postman, parcel
Good morning. I want to send this package.
Sure. Let me put it in a box and weigh it to see how heavy it is. It is a little heavy. Where is it going?
It is going to another country, to Spain.
Do you want fast or slow delivery?
Fast, please. How many days does it take?
About four days. Please write the address here, and the sender's name too.
Done. I also have this letter and a postcard. Do they need stamps?
Yes, I'll add a stamp and put the letter in an envelope. I'll close it with tape.
Here you are. Can I have a receipt? Thank you. Where do I drop future letters to mail?
There is a mailbox outside, and the postman collects it every day. Your parcel and letter will go out soon.
