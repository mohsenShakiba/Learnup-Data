A Festival Calendar

Our town has many festivals.
Really? When do they start?
The music festival is in March.
Is there anything in February?
Yes, a small flower festival in February.
Nice. What about spring and summer?
There is an art fair in April and a book festival in May. Then a big one in June and fireworks in July.
I love the big summer crowd.
And a food festival in August with a street parade.
I love autumn festivals too.
Yes, one in September and one in October.
Is there a winter program?
In November we have a show with dancing. And in December and January, light festivals.
Wow, there are festivals in many months!
