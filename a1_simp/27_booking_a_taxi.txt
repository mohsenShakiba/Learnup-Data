Hello, I'd like to book a taxi, please.
Sure. Where do you want to go?
To the airport. My flight is at nine.
What time should we pick you up?
Early, please. At six in the morning.
Okay. What is your address for the pickup?
12 Park Road. I have two bags.
No problem. Your cab will be a blue car waiting outside.
Good. There can be a lot of traffic.
Don't worry, six is early. There's no need to hurry.
Which way will the driver go?
The driver takes the highway, then the fast lane past the roundabout.
Does the meter start at pickup?
Yes. Please put on your seatbelt in the car.
Sure. I hope there is no traffic jam.
If a car honks, the driver will brake safely. We have plenty of fuel.
Great. I confirmed the booking. I'll be ready.
