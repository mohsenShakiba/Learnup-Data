Hello. What seems to be the problem?
My tooth really hurts.
Please open your mouth wide. Let me check your teeth.
I feel a lot of pain on this side.
I see the problem. This tooth has a cavity, a small hole with decay.
Is it serious?
No, but I need to fill it with a filling today. I'll use a drill.
Will it hurt?
I'll give you an injection to make it numb, and I'll be careful and gentle.
Thank you. What can I do at home?
Brush twice a day, floss to keep your gums clean, and rinse your mouth.
Okay. And less sweet food?
Yes, eat less sweet food. Come back to the dentist in six months.
Okay, I will. Thank you, doctor.
