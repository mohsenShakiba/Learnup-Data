A Strange Dream
strange, dark, awake, ugly, fat, piece, gold, forget, dream, asleep, scary, nightmare, monster, ghost, giant, chase, hide, shadow, deep, magic, weird
I had a strange dream last night.
Really? Tell me about it.
It was dark and a little scary, like a nightmare.
Were you awake or asleep?
Asleep. I saw an ugly monster and a ghost.
Was it big and fat?
Yes, a giant! It held a piece of gold.
Then what happened?
It began to chase me, so I tried to hide.
Did you see its shadow?
Yes, in the deep dark. It felt like magic.
Then what?
I forget the rest of it.
Dreams are strange and weird like that.
Yes. I don't want to sleep now.
Don't worry, it was not real.
