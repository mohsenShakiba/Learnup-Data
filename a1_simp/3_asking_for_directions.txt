Asking for Directions
excuse, help, bank, street, far, near, minute, walk, straight, turn, left, corner, right, lost, sorry, follow, road, block, cross, behind, bus stop, across
Excuse me, sorry to bother you. Can you help me?
Sure. What are you looking for?
I'm looking for the bank.
It's on Main Street. Just follow this road.
Is it far?
No, it's near. It's two blocks away, about five minutes.
How do I get there?
Walk straight, then cross the street at the corner.
Left or right after that?
Turn left. The bank is behind the bus stop.
Is it near the park?
Yes, it's right across from the park.
Thank you so much. I was lost.
No problem. Take care!
