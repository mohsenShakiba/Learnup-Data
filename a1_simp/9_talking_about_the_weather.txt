Talking About the Weather
weather, sunny, jacket, warm, summer, spring, rain, umbrella, sky, cloud, cloudy, wind, wet, degrees, season, winter, snow, ice, autumn, fog, storm
Good morning! The weather's nice today.
Yes, it is very sunny. The sky is blue.
It was cold and cloudy yesterday.
I know. I needed a jacket, and the streets were still wet from the rain.
It's warm today, about twenty-five degrees. I like it.
Me too. Summer is coming.
Do you like hot weather?
Not really. Spring is my favorite season.
What about winter? Do you like the snow and ice?
The snow is pretty, but ice on the roads is not fun. And I don't like the autumn fog.
They say a storm will come tomorrow, with clouds and wind.
Then I'll bring an umbrella.
Good idea. Have a great day!
You too!
