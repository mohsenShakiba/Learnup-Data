Talking About Family
family, brother, sister, older, younger, live, mother, father, cousin, city, married, children, son, daughter, visit, grandfather, grandmother, aunt, uncle, wife, husband
Do you have a big family?
Yes, I have two brothers and one sister.
That's nice. Are they older than you?
My brothers are older. My sister is younger.
Do they live near you?
My sister lives with my mother and father. My brothers live in another city.
Does your grandfather or uncle live with you too?
No, but my grandmother, aunt, and cousins visit us every week.
Are you married?
Yes, my wife and I have two children.
A son and a daughter?
Yes! How about you?
My husband and I have one daughter. She is five.
Maybe you can visit us sometime.
That sounds nice.
