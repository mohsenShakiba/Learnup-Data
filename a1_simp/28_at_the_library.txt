Hi, I want to borrow some books.
Are you a member?
No, not yet. How can I join?
It's free. I just need your name and address.
Here you are. Where can I find the history books?
In that section, near the window. The librarian can help you.
Thank you. I also want a novel and a book of poems.
The fiction section has novels. Do you like biography or poems?
Biography, please. Who is a good author?
This one. Check the catalog for more titles.
Great. Can I also get a textbook to study?
Yes. With this library card you can borrow five books.
When should I return them? Can I renew?
In three weeks. You can renew once. Please don't be late.
What happens if I am late?
You pay a small late fee. Please keep silence and only whisper inside.
Okay. This novel has a nice cover and many chapters.
