Buying a Bus Ticket

Hi. I need a ticket to London, please.
Single or return?
Single, please. When does the next coach depart from the station?
It leaves at ten. The full schedule is on this timetable.
How much does it cost? What is the fare?
It is twelve dollars.
Can I pay by card?
Sorry, we only take cash today.
Okay. Here is twenty dollars. Which platform is it?
Platform three. Here is your change, and your seat is number eight.
Is it a long journey?
About three hours. The route goes through two towns.
Can I bring my backpack on board?
Yes, every passenger can bring one bag. The coach has a big engine, so it's fast.
Great. When does it arrive?
At one o'clock. The bus stops once on the way.
