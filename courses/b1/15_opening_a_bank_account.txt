Opening a Bank Account
current, interest, documents, statement, apply, fees, overdraft, minimum, banking, cheque, chequebook, withdrawal, eligible, joint, contactless, notify, charges, funds, cashpoint, sort code
Good morning. I'd like to apply for an account.
Of course. A savings or current account?
What's the difference between them?
A savings account pays more interest.
Are there any monthly fees or charges?
No, but keep a minimum balance to avoid an overdraft.
Can I manage it with online banking?
Yes, and you get a contactless card too.
What documents do I need?
Your identity card, proof of address, and your sort code.
Can we open a joint account together?
Certainly, if you are both eligible.
Do I get a chequebook?
Yes, so you can write a cheque when needed.
How do I take out money?
Use any cashpoint for a quick withdrawal.
We'll notify you when the funds are ready.
You can also download every statement online.
