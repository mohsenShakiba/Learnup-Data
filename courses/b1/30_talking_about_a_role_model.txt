Talking About a Role Model
role model, inspire, admire, values, determined, overcome, humble, dedicated, idol, modest, respected, driven, nurturing, sacrificed, fearless, gracious, disciplined, grit, tenacity, down-to-earth
Who has influenced you the most in life?
Without a doubt, my grandmother. She was my greatest role model.
What did you admire about her?
She was determined, driven, and endlessly dedicated.
Did she overcome difficult times?
Yes, she sacrificed everything for us.
That must have taken real grit.
It did. She faced hardship with fearless tenacity.
And yet she stayed humble and modest.
People must have respected her.
Everyone did. She was so gracious to all.
She sounds nurturing too.
Very. She was warm but disciplined with us.
What did she teach you?
To live by strong values and stay down-to-earth.
She truly does inspire me every day.
She sounds like a real role model.
