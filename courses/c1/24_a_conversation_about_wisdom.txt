A Conversation About Wisdom
wisdom, knowledge, discernment, judgment, accumulate, reflection, prudent, insight, distinguish, mature, wisdom practice, lived experience, good judgment, cleverness, foolishness, life lesson, reflective practice, intellectual humility, curious, choose with care
Is wisdom just accumulated knowledge?
No, knowledge is knowing; wisdom is judging.
So you can be knowledgeable but unwise?
Certainly. Cleverness can coexist with foolishness.
Then how is wisdom actually acquired?
Largely through reflection, lived experience, and reflective practice.
So mistakes are essential to it?
Indispensable, provided each life lesson becomes good judgment.
It seems to require real humility.
Yes, intellectual humility and knowing the limits of what you know.
That's a discernment few truly master as a wisdom practice.
Which is why wise people stay curious and choose with care.
