The growth model assumes the planet will absorb every external cost.
That assumption is collapsing under climate risk and biodiversity loss.
A carbon budget makes the limit visible.
Yes, but planetary limits are broader than carbon.
Resource depletion and ecological thresholds interact in unpredictable ways.
Systems thinking helps us see that overshoot in one area weakens resilience elsewhere.
Where does environmental justice fit?
At the center. The people least responsible often face the harshest adaptation costs.
Some argue for degrowth, others for a circular economy.
Either way, consumption has to face an ecological ceiling.
Sustainable development cannot mean infinite expansion with better branding.
Long-term survival requires respecting interdependence before crisis forces it.
