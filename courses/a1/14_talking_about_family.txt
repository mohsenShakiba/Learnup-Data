Talking About Family
family, mother, father, sister, brother, son, daughter, aunt, uncle, cousin, parent, grandmother, grandfather, husband, wife, child, fill, picture, place
Is this a picture of your family?
Yes. These are my parents, my mother and my father.
Do you have a sister or a brother?
I have one sister and one brother.
Who is the woman next to your grandfather?
That is my grandmother. Here is my aunt and her husband, my uncle.
Are these their children?
Yes, their son and daughter. They are my cousins.
Your family is big!
