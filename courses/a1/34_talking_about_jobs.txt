Talking About Jobs

How's the new job going? You started last month, right?
It's good. The work is hard, but the staff are friendly and the salary is fair.
That's great. I remember you were nervous before the interview.
I was. I stayed up until it was dark, reading over my resume again and again.
Well, it paid off. This could really help your career.
I hope so. They liked that I had the right skills for the role.
Did you sign the contract yet?
Yes, this morning. I should get my first pay in dollars, because the company is American.
Nice! So you'll often work with people abroad?
Often, yes. But right now I just need to buy a coffee — the machine by the bathroom broke, and I can't stand a morning without one.
