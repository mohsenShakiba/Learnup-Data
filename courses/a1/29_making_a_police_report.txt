Good evening. I'd like to make a police report.
Of course. I'm the policeman on duty. What happened?
I saw something dangerous on this road yesterday.
What time was it?
It was dark, but there was a light above the bank door.
What did you see?
I saw a man take a woman's bag.
Did anyone else see it?
No. Nobody was near us.
Did you hear anything?
The woman called for someone to help her.
Please tell me what you remember about the man.
He was tall and had dark hair.
Can you describe his clothes?
He wore a long black coat and blue jeans.
Did you see his face?
Not well. He had something over his nose and mouth.
What colour were his eyes?
I think each eye was brown.
Was the woman tall or short?
She was short, with blonde hair.
Did the man say anything?
No, he ran away.
Thank you. The police will check the area.
I hope you find him.
