Listening to the Weather News
radio, news, report, rain, snow, cold, wet, dry, north, south, east, west, country, city, town, mountain, river, island, road, traffic, dangerous, drive, air, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, home, prepare
Turn up the radio. The weather news is starting.
We need the report before we drive across the country.
“On Monday, heavy rain will fall in the north.”
That could make the road wet.
“And snow will reach the eastern mountains on Tuesday.”
Cold air is coming from the east.
What about the south?
“The south will be warm and dry on Wednesday.”
That's where we're going.
But we have to pass through a city near the river.
Listen. They're talking about that area now.
“Traffic will be slow in the city centre.”
Is it dangerous to drive there?
Only if the rain is very heavy.
What does the report say about the west?
“Western towns will be clear on Thursday.”
And the island?
“It will stay dry until Friday.”
Good. We can prepare for rain and cold before we leave.
Should we take the mountain road?
No. Let's stay on the main road.
When will we be home?
On Friday evening.
Then our trip should be safe.
Yes. Now let's check the map.
