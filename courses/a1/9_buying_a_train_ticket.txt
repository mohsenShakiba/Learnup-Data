Buying a Train Ticket
train, ticket, station, tomorrow, morning, return, cost, o'clock, leave, arrive
Hello. I'd like a train ticket, please.
Where are you going?
To Brighton tomorrow morning.
What time would you like to leave?
At nine o'clock, please.
That train will arrive at ten thirty.
Do you want a return ticket?
Yes, I want to come back in the evening.
It costs twenty pounds.
Great. Can I pay by card?
Yes. Here is your ticket. The train leaves from this station.
