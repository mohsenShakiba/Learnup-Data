At the Train Station
platform, train, three, delay, local, track, carriage, journey, station, second, rail, always, first
Excuse me, which platform does the train to London leave from?
Platform three, but there's a short delay this morning.
Oh no. How long is the delay? I have a meeting at noon.
About twenty minutes. A local train is blocking the track ahead.
I see. Is this the fast service, or does it stop everywhere?
This is the fast one. The first carriage is quieter if you want to rest.
That's good. It's a long journey and I'd like to read.
The station café is open if you need a coffee while you wait.
Does the second train also go to London?
Yes, but this rail line is quicker. Always take the first one if you can.
