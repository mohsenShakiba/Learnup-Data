At the Train Station
train, express, local, announcement, direct, carriage, compartment, conductor, lounge, waiting, escalator, entrance, trolley, refreshment, exit, railway, speed, tunnel, whistle, rail
Excuse me, I've got a ticket to Paris. When's the next train?
It leaves at three. It's the fast express, not the local one.
Oh good. Which platform?
Platform five. It's running a little late though, so listen for the announcement.
Late? How late are we talking?
Just ten minutes or so. Don't worry.
Is it a direct train, or do I change somewhere?
You'll change in Lyon. So no, it's not direct.
Okay. And which carriage is my seat?
Carriage four, a nice quiet compartment. The conductor will check your ticket on board.
Right. Is there somewhere I can wait? A lounge, maybe?
Yes, the waiting lounge is just up the escalator, near the entrance.
Perfect. Can I grab a coffee up there?
Sure, there's a trolley with refreshments, or a café by the exit.
Nice. This railway is new, right? Is it part of the high-speed rail service?
Yes. It travels at high speed through the long tunnel.
Great, thanks. I'll head to the platform now.
When you hear the whistle, the train doors are about to close. Have a good trip!
