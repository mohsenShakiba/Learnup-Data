A Walk in Nature

What a beautiful day to be outside. Look at all these tall trees!
This forest is my favourite place. In winter it's quiet, but today the birds are singing.
Listen to them. And that little river down the hill — I can hear the water.
There's a path that follows it. Be careful, the leaves are wet and slippery.
I don't mind. Can we climb all the way up the mountain?
We can, but it's a long line of steps — about seventy, I think.
I'll manage. Are there many tourists on this path?
Not today. Sometimes the police close it after a storm, so people stay away.
Good for us, then. Look, a child is playing over there by the flowers.
Yes. It costs only a few cents to park, so families come out and use the forest all year.
