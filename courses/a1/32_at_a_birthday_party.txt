At a Birthday Party
birthday, party, cake, candle, present, gift, balloon, wish, celebrate, now, August, pair, mean, city, pound, forty, beer, which, hour, newspaper
Happy birthday! I can't believe you're forty now — an August baby, just like me.
It means so much that you all came out for this party.
Of course. The whole city seems to be here! Who blew up all these balloons?
My brother did. It took him an hour, and there are still more to fill.
Look at that cake — how many candles are on it?
Forty, if you can believe it. My niece wanted one for every year.
Here, this is for you. It's just a small present.
You didn't have to bring a gift. What is it?
A pair of shoes and a book I read about in the newspaper. It only cost a few pounds.
I love it. Now, which would you like — some cake or a cold beer?
A cold beer, please. Then we can all celebrate. I wish you a wonderful year.
