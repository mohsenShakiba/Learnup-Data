Planning a Town Festival
festival, event, town, local, people, everyone, activity, programme, food, band, singer, game, child, adult, prepare, table, chair, start, finish, July, summer, rain, meeting, discuss, idea, share, free, market, culture, popular
Thanks for coming to this meeting.
We need to discuss the town festival.
The event will be on the first Saturday in July.
A summer festival is a great idea.
Where should it start?
In the local market at ten in the morning.
What activities should we put in the programme?
We need a game for every child.
And we can have an art activity for each adult.
Will there be food?
Yes. People from the market can prepare local dishes.
We should put a table and chairs near each food area.
What about music?
A popular band can play in the afternoon.
I know a singer who would like to join them.
That will help us share our culture with visitors.
Should everyone pay to enter?
No, the festival will be free.
How will we finish the day?
With music and dancing at eight.
What if there is rain?
We can move the activities into the town hall.
Good. I'll prepare a list of jobs.
And I'll share the new programme with everyone.
