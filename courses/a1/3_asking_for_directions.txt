Asking for Directions
station, map, street, left, right, near, walk, bus, where, help
Excuse me, can you help me?
Of course. Where do you want to go?
I need to go to the train station.
The station is near here.
Should I take the bus?
No, you can walk.
Go down this street and turn left at the bank.
Then turn right at the cafe.
Can you show me on this map?
Yes. The station is here.
Thank you very much.
You're welcome.
