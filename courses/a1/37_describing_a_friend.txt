Have you met Tom's new girlfriend yet? I met her a week ago.
Not yet. What is she like as a person?
Oh, she's lovely — tall, with bright red hair and a very cheerful smile.
Tom always said he wanted to meet someone kind.
She is. Gentle and polite, but also clever and very funny.
That sounds perfect for him. Is she shy?
A little at first, but once you begin talking she opens up. And she's honest, which I like.
Being honest matters. Not many people are so open these days.
You'll see when you meet her. We all watched a DVD together and played ball in the garden — she fit right in.
I'd like that. She sounds brave to join such a loud group so soon. Maybe we'll plan another evening.
Let's do it. You two will get on well.
