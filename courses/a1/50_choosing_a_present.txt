Choosing a Present for a Teenager
teenager, boy, girl, choose, idea, clothes, T-shirt, jeans, shoe, CD, DVD, game, football, ball, ride, bike, camera, computer, magazine, chocolate, money, cheap, colour, interested, interest, fun, cool, something, else, perfect
I need to choose something for my nephew.
How old is he?
He's a fifteen-year-old teenager.
What is he interested in?
He has a strong interest in football.
Then a new ball could be fun.
That's a good idea, but he already has one.
Does the boy like to ride a bike?
Yes, but a new bike costs too much money.
What about clothes?
A cool T-shirt or jeans could work.
Do you know his shoe size?
No, so shoes may be difficult.
Does he like music?
Yes. We could get him a CD.
Or maybe a DVD or computer game.
He plays many games already.
Is he interested in taking pictures?
Yes! A small camera would be perfect.
This one is cheap and comes in every colour.
I'll choose the black camera.
Would you like anything else?
Maybe a magazine about photography.
And some chocolate?
Good idea. His sister is a little girl, so I'll get chocolate for her too.
Now you have the perfect presents.
