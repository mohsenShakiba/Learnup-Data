A Day on the Farm
farm, farmer, cow, pig, sheep, horse, animal, bird, plant, grow, fruit, vegetable, apple, carrot, food, country, outside, air, summer, spring, autumn, winter, rain, snow, wet, dry, carry, boot, jacket, cold
Welcome to my uncle's farm.
It's beautiful. I love being in the country.
The air is so clean here.
My uncle is a farmer, and he works outside every day.
What animal can we see first?
Those are cows, and the pigs are behind them.
I can also see a sheep and a horse.
Look! A small bird is sitting on the wall.
Does your uncle grow food here too?
Yes. Every plant in this garden is useful.
What kind of fruit does he grow?
Mostly apples. You can take an apple from that tree.
What kind of vegetable does he grow?
We have carrots near the house. You can try this carrot.
Can I help carry this box of carrots?
Yes, please. Put on your boots first.
One boot is already wet from the grass.
Why? The sun is out.
We had a lot of rain this morning, so the ground is wet.
Good idea. I'll get my jacket too because it's cold.
It will be warm and dry later.
Is the farm busy all year?
Yes. Plants begin to grow in spring.
The animals stay outside in summer.
We collect fruit and vegetables in autumn.
And what happens in winter?
There is often snow, so the animals stay inside.
Farm life changes with every season.
