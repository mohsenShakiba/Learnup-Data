Buying a Bus Ticket
bus, ticket, driver, town, pound, money, leave, past, far, away, near, stop, next, market, travel, people
Hello. A ticket to town, please.
Of course. That is two pounds.
Here is the money. When does the next bus leave?
At half past nine.
Thank you. Is the town far away?
No, it is quite near.
Good. Where do I get off?
The driver will tell you the stop. It is next to the market.
