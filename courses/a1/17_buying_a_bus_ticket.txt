Buying a Bus Ticket
bus, driver, ticket, town, next, front, far, near, course, come, open, close, o'clock, past, how, travel, away
Hello, driver. A ticket to town, please.
Here you are. The doors are open now.
Thank you. When does the next bus come?
This one comes at half past nine.
And how far is the town?
It is not far away. It is near.
Good. Can I sit at the front?
Yes, of course.
I like to travel by bus.
Me too. Please close the door — we leave at ten o'clock.
Thank you. Have a good day.
