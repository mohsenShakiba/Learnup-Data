At the Post Office
post, letter, send, address, country, anything, box, price, euro, money, pay, put, then, week, arrive
Hello. I want to send this letter.
Where is it going?
To another country. Here is the address.
Do you want to send anything else?
Yes. Can you post this box too?
Of course. The price for the box is three euros.
That is fine. I will pay for both. Here is the money.
Put them here, then. The letter takes about a week to arrive.
