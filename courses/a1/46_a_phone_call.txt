Hi! Can you hear me? Let me turn off the speaker.

Oh yes, the trip. What time on Thursday?
The boat leaves at seven in the morning from the north pier.
Seven! That's early. How long is the ride?
About twelve hours. We get in around nineteen hundred — seven at night.
A whole day at sea! I also get a little seasick, but I'm happy to try it.
You'll be fine. There'll be about sixty of us on board, one trip each month.
I can't wait. My cold is nearly gone, so my nose won't trouble me.
Good. I'll send a message with the details, then I have to hang up — see you at home on Thursday.
