Reporting a Lost Wallet
police, stolen, happened, cards, leather, officer, details, theft, robbery, pickpocket, victim, description, suspect, crime, incident, missing, identification, fraud, blocked, emergency
Hello, officer. I'd like to report a missing wallet.
I see. When did it happen?
It happened about an hour ago, on the bus.
So it may be theft?
Maybe a pickpocket. It could be a robbery.
Can you give a description?
It's brown leather, quite small.
What was inside?
Some cash and two bank cards.
You should get the cards blocked to stop fraud.
Good idea. I've cancelled them already.
Do the police need more details?
Yes, please describe the suspect.
I didn't see who had stolen it.
This is a common crime. I'll log the incident.
Am I the only victim?
No. We take every theft seriously.
Do you need my identification?
Yes, for the report. Call our emergency line if anything else goes missing.
