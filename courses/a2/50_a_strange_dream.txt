A Strange Dream
strange, dark, awake, ugly, fat, piece, gold, forget, dream, asleep, scary, nightmare, monster, ghost, giant, chase, hide, shadow, deep, magic, weird
I had such a strange dream last night.
Really? Tell me about it.
It was dark and scary, like a real nightmare.
Were you awake or asleep?
Asleep. There was this ugly monster, and a ghost too.
Was it big and fat?
Huge, a giant! And it was holding a piece of gold.
Really? Then what?
It started to chase me, so I tried to hide.
Did you see its shadow?
Yes, in the deep dark. It felt like magic somehow.
And then?
I forget the rest.
Dreams are so strange and weird like that.
I know. I kind of don't want to sleep now.
Come on, it wasn't real.
