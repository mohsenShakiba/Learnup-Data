Joining a Sports Club

Hi, I'm Rana. I would like to join the sports club, but I am not sure which activity to choose.
Hello, Rana. I'm Hossein. We have soccer, hockey, baseball, and a running group.
I enjoy running, but my ankle hurt after my last race.
I am sorry to hear that. Has your knee felt all right since then?
Yes, it has. The doctor said I can exercise again if I start slowly.
Then the running group may be a good choice. We meet regularly on the field near the school.
What happens during a usual session?
The coach helps each runner improve their average speed and stay fit.
Do you ever enter races together?
Yes. Last season, one of our athletes won a prize in the city race.
That is impressive. I do not expect to win, but I would like to succeed at finishing five kilometres.
That is an excellent goal. Everyone has a chance to improve with practice.
What about the team sports? I have never played hockey.
Hockey is fast, but beginners are welcome. In soccer and baseball, we also keep a friendly score.
Can I try more than one sport before I decide?
Certainly. The club gives new members two free sessions.
Great. I will try running first and see how my ankle feels.
Good plan. I hope to see you at the field on Wednesday.
