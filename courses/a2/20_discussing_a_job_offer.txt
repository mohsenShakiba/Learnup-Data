Discussing a Job Offer
employ, employer, salary, department, role, position, earn, degree, apply, boss, successful, education, advantage, increase
Hi, I'm Shabnam. I received a job offer this morning, and I would like your advice.
Hello, Shabnam. I'm Mehrdad. Congratulations. What is the position?
It is a marketing role in a small business that employs about twenty people.
That sounds promising. What does the employer expect from you?
I would work in the sales department and report directly to the boss.
Do they offer a regular salary?
Yes, and they said it could increase after six months if I meet my goals.
That is a real advantage. Does the role match your degree?
Mostly. My education was in business management, but I have not worked in sales before.
Could you apply your previous customer-service experience there?
I think so. They want someone who can speak clearly and organize tasks.
That makes sense. How much would you earn compared with your current job?
The salary is better, but the office is farther from home.
Would the new position help you build a successful career?
I believe it would. The company is growing, and there may be more responsibility later.
Then it seems worth serious thought. What is making you hesitate?
I am comfortable where I am, but I do not want to miss a good chance.
Give yourself a day to decide. You have earned the right to choose carefully.
