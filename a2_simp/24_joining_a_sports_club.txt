Joining a Sports Club
beginner, equipment, match, fitness, tennis, membership, racket, session, tournament, warmup, opponent, court, athletic, instructor, sporty, fixture, teammates, referee, league, trainers
Hi, I'd like to join the tennis club.
Welcome! Have you played before?
No, I'm a complete beginner.
No problem. Our instructor coaches beginners.
Great. When is the training session?
Twice a week. We warmup first, then practise.
How much is the membership?
Forty dollars a month, trainers not included.
Do I need my own equipment?
We can lend you a racket at first.
When is my first match?
There's a friendly fixture next Sunday.
Will I have teammates?
Yes, teammates beside you and an opponent across the net.
Who controls the match?
A referee for every game.
Is there a league?
Yes, and a summer tournament on our best court.
I want to feel athletic and fit.
You'll improve your fitness. See you soon, future sporty star!
