Describing a Person
tall, beard, wearing, recognize, polite, smile, remember
Do you remember my friend Mark?
I'm not sure. Can you describe him?
He's tall with short dark hair.
Does he wear glasses?
Yes, and he has a short beard.
What is he like?
He's very friendly and polite.
Is he the one who is a bit shy?
Yes, but he has a big smile.
Now I remember him!
He'll be at the party tonight.
Great. I'll recognize him now.
