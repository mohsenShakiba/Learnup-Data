Do you remember my friend Mark?
I'm not sure. Can you describe his appearance?
He's tall and slim, not chubby at all.
Does he have much hair?
No, he's nearly bald, with thick eyebrows.
Any beard?
A small moustache and short sideburns.
What about his face?
He has freckles, dimples, and high cheekbones.
Hmm, does he resemble anyone?
A bit. He has a tanned complexion.
Is he the muscular type?
Yes, quite stocky and strong.
What was he wearing last time?
A scruffy coat, with his hair in a fringe.
Now I remember him!
The one with a few wrinkles and a ponytail?
That's him. You'll recognize him tonight.
