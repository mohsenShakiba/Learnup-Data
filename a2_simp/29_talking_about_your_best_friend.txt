Talking About Your Best Friend
met, school, funny, trust, support, similar, honest, memory, close
Tell me about your best friend.
Her name is Lena. We met at school.
How long have you known her?
For more than ten years.
What do you like about her?
She's funny, kind, and very honest.
Do you have a lot in common?
Yes, we share similar interests.
Can you trust her with anything?
Absolutely. She always supports me.
You're lucky to have such a close friend.
I really am. We have so many good memories.
