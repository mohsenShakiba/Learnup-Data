Talking About Your Best Friend

Tell me about your best friend.
Her name is Lena. We met at school.
How long have you known her?
Ten years. We're almost inseparable.
What do you like about her?
She's honest, caring, and full of humour.
Do you share the same hobbies?
Yes, we have similar interests.
Is she a good companion?
The best. Our bond is very strong.
Can you trust her?
Completely. She's trustworthy and dependable.
Is she a good listener?
Very understanding, and always sincere.
Does she support you?
Yes, she's supportive and devoted.
Can you confide in her?
Always. I value her friendship and companionship.
You're lucky to have her.
I am. She's considerate and I trust her fully.
