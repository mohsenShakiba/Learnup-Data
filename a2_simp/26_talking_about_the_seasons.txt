Which season do you like best?

What's your favorite, then?

That's true. But the days get shorter.
I don't mind. I like cool weather.
Do you like winter and snow?
Sometimes, but it's often too cold.
In spring, I love working in the garden.
Spring is nice too. Everything is fresh.
