Talking About Your Hometown
hometown, grew, changed, bigger, factory, memories, moved, still, village, population, neighbourhood, childhood, outskirts, industrial, harbour, castle, riverside, surroundings, lively, traditional, residents, cottages
Where did you grow up?
I grew up in a small village by a harbour.
Is it your hometown?
Yes. I have happy childhood memories there.
Has it changed much?
A lot. The population is much bigger now.
How is the neighbourhood today?
It's lively, with new shops on the outskirts.
Did the old industrial area change too?
Yes, the factory closed, but the riverside is beautiful.
There's also an old castle above the town.
Do people still live in the old cottages?
Some residents do. It still feels traditional.
Why did you leave?
My family moved for work, but I love the surroundings.
Would you go back?
Maybe one day. It will always be my home.
