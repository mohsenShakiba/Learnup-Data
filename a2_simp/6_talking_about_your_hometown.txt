Talking About Your Hometown
hometown, grew, changed, bigger, factory, memories, moved, still
Where did you grow up?
I grew up in a small town by a river.
That sounds peaceful. Was it quiet?
Yes, very quiet. But it has changed a lot.
How is it different now?
It's much bigger, and there is a new factory.
Do you still have family there?
Yes, my parents still live there.
Do you visit often?
A few times a year. I miss it sometimes.
I'm sure you have good memories there.
I do. It will always be my home.
