Talking About Pets
feed, playful, fur, responsibility, rescue, company, adopt, love, kitten, hamster, goldfish, cage, litter, obedient, affectionate, feeding, cuddle, purr, wag, fetch
Do you have any pets?
Yes, a cat, a dog, and a hamster.
That's lovely. Are they playful?
The dog loves to fetch, but the cat likes to purr.
Do you feed them yourself?
Yes, feeding them is a big responsibility.
Is the cat affectionate?
Very. She cuddles me and I stroke her fur.
Do you clean up after them?
I change the litter and the cage every day.
Are they well trained?
The dog is obedient. He learns to wag on command.
Any other animals?
A goldfish and a kitten, full of love.
Where did you get them?
From a rescue center. They give me company.
Should I get one?
Yes, adopt one! Pets bring joy.
