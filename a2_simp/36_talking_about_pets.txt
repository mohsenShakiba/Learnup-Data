Talking About Pets
pet, cat, feed, playful, fur, responsibility, rescue, company, adopt, love
Do you have any pets?
Yes, I have a cat and a dog.
That's lovely. Are they playful?
The dog is, but the cat is quiet.
Do you walk the dog every day?
Yes, twice a day. It's a big responsibility.
Where did you get them?
I adopted them from a rescue center.
That's wonderful. They must be happy.
They are. They give me great company.
Maybe I should adopt a pet too.
You should! They bring so much love.
