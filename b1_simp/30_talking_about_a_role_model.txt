Talking About a Role Model
role model, inspire, admire, values, determined, overcome, humble, example, dedicated, lesson
Who has influenced you the most in life?
Without a doubt, my grandmother.
What did you admire about her?
She was incredibly determined.
Did she overcome difficult times?
Yes, she raised five children alone.
That's truly inspiring.
And yet she stayed so humble.
What's the biggest lesson she taught you?
To never give up on my values.
She sounds like a wonderful person.
She was. I'm so grateful for her example.
