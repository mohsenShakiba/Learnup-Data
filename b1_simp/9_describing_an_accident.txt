Describing an Accident
accident, suddenly, driving, crossing, crash, injured, witness
Can you tell me what happened?
I was driving home when it happened.
Where exactly were you?
I was crossing the bridge near the station.
What did the other driver do?
He suddenly stopped, so I couldn't brake in time.
Was anyone injured?
No, luckily we were both fine.
Was there much damage?
Just the front of my car.
Were there any witnesses?
Yes, a man saw it and gave a statement.
