Describing an Accident
accident, suddenly, driving, crossing, crash, injured, witness, stopped, luckily, collision, swerve, bumper, ambulance, shock, careless, skid, speeding, whiplash, windscreen, tow
Can you tell me what happened?
I was driving home when the accident happened.
Where exactly were you?
I was crossing the bridge near the station.
What did the other driver do?
He was speeding and suddenly stopped in front of me.
So you couldn't avoid the crash?
No. I tried to swerve, but I hit his bumper.
Did the cars skid on the road?
A little. It was a low-speed collision.
Was anyone injured?
Luckily not, though my neck aches from whiplash.
That can be delayed shock. Did you call for help?
Yes, an ambulance came to check us both.
Was there much damage?
The windscreen cracked, so they had to tow my car.
Were there any witnesses?
Yes, a man saw the careless driving and gave a statement.
