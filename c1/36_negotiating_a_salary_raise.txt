Thanks for meeting me. I'd like to discuss a salary bump.
I appreciate you raising it. What's prompting the raise request?
Frankly, I've felt underpaid relative to the market rate for a while.
Do you have a benchmark? Something comparable to point to?
Yes — my counterpart at a rival firm earns well above my pay band.
Our bonus structure and equity grant do offset the base, though.
Understood, but my last performance review was excellent, and a raise feels overdue.
I won't lowball you. Give me a ballpark figure.
A ten-percent merit based increase, plus a cost of living adjustment.
The merit part I can defend given your track record and contributions.
Is that number nonnegotiable on your side?
Not entirely. Let's revisit it alongside your promotion track next quarter.
