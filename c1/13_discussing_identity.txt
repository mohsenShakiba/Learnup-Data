Exactly. It evolves with ancestry, hybridity, and cultural memory.
Still, I struggle to reconcile both sides without alienation.
Maybe the duality and code switching are themselves a strength.
That's a generous way of seeing rootedness.
Belonging can come from home making within, not just place.

Precisely. Identity is something you author.
