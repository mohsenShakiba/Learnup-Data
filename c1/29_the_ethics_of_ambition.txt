When does ambition cross an ethical line?
When it starts trampling on others; that's the ethical line.
So ambition itself isn't the problem?
Not at all. Principled ambition can be quite noble.
But some pursue success ruthlessly.
Abandoning their principles along the way.
They tell themselves success at any cost justifies it.
That's a dangerously seductive rationalization.
Can you stay ambitious and principled?
Absolutely, if you keep your conscience and moral compass intact.
